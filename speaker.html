May 16th, 2019

 

Quentin Sa’Lay

Comcast/ NBC Universal

Baby Boomers and Generation X

Quentin Sa’Lay is a global HR executive, supporting technology leaders in the US, India, China, Singapore, and the United Kingdom for Comcast NBC Universal. He offers a unique blend of executive acumen, critical evaluation skills, ethical leadership coupled with an earned reputation for excellence. He is a sought-after executive coach to C-Suite executives and a Prosci Change Management professional. Quentin is a graduate of the NAMIC Executive Leadership Development Program from The Darden School of Business at the University of Virginia, He received certification in Human Resource Management from Cornell University School of Industrial and Labor Relations. Quentin holds a BS in Psychology and Human Resource Management from Towson University, and a Master of Science in Social Psychology at University of Maryland. Quentin is a 2015 inductee in the National Society of Collegiate Scholars and a certified Executive Coach with the International Institute of Professional and Executive Coaches.

Keynote Luncheon Speaker:
Eddie DeJesus

Representing Millennials and Generation Z

 

Fannie Mae

Eddie DeJesus is an Innovation Pilot Manager at The Fannie Mae Corporation. He specializes in managing advanced analytics pilots, primarily in AI/machine learning. He is a graduate of the University of Maryland: College Park and will be graduating with his MBA from Georgetown University in May of 2019, with a focus in consumer analytics. At Georgetown, Eddie is the founder of Hoyalytics, the Georgetown Data Analytics Club. He loves solving problems with data and is a self-proclaimed data nerd.
